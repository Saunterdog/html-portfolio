The Best Movies According to Alexi

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Forrest Gump

Funny, heartbreaking and remarkably loving

Spirited Away, Castle In The Sky and Princess Monokoke

Impossible to choose between the three, these incredible stories have clung to my soul. Plus, I really enjoy the food scenes.

Star Wars

The science fiction/fantasy mashup that defined a generation and then the ones that followed.
